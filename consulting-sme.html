What I do

Four ways to work together

Most engagements start with a short discovery conversation. From there, we choose the right scope.

01

Business Discovery

A focused conversation about what is working, what is not, and what assumptions your team has stopped questioning. Most clients leave with a different understanding of their problem than when they arrived.

02

Diagnostic Consulting

A structured audit of your current processes, tools, and team. Written findings, a clear priority list, and specific next steps — not a slide deck full of generics.

03

Market Expansion

Research and strategy for moving into a new customer segment or geography. Grounded in what your existing customers actually value, not what your competitors claim to offer.

04

Process Improvement

Mapping and simplifying the workflows that cost your team the most time. Finding where AI saves real hours — and where it would just add noise.

The first conversation is free. And honest.

No pitch, no proposal until we both agree there is something worth working on. You will leave with at least one useful observation.

What I do

Diagnose. Build. Hand off cleanly.

The same loop every time, whether the engagement is two weeks or six months.

01 · Diagnose

Find the actual problem

I have entered a new industry with zero domain experience more times than most people change jobs: retail, print, telecom, eCommerce, non-profit. The skill that transfers is framing. Before anything gets built, we agree in plain language on what is actually broken and what fixing it is worth.

02 · Build

Ship working software

Not a slide deck about software. Small, focused tools that solve the diagnosed problem: intake forms that actually route, checklists that actually get used, diagnostics your customers can run themselves. Built lean, tested against the live thing, delivered working.

Digital literacy

Digital Confidence Centre

Live · free · no account required

The problem
Canadian seniors are the primary target of online scams, and most digital literacy material talks down to them or requires sign-ups they do not trust.
What was built
A free self-paced digital literacy platform: 29 adult modules covering scam protection, online banking, video calls, and AI, plus 25 or more caregiver-led activities for kids. Accessible design, no login, no data collected.
Why it matters as proof
It shows the full loop on a hard audience: diagnosing what actually blocks seniors, building for accessibility rather than around it, and shipping something a library can hand to a patron today.

AI readiness diagnostic

Job search tooling

Career Coach

Live · free · your data stays in your browser

The problem
Job seekers burn hours tailoring applications blind, with no honest read on fit, and most tools want their data on someone else's server.
What was built
An AI-powered job application tool: it analyses your CV against real postings and scores ATS match, fit, salary alignment, and AI displacement risk. Everything runs in your browser with your own API key. Nothing is stored on a server.
Why it matters as proof
Privacy-first architecture as a design decision, not a compliance afterthought. The same sovereignty standard is applied to anything built for a client.

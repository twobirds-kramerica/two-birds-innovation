New direction to explore — the "2B" mark

Aaron's brief: numeral 2 + a B stylized to read as two birds, "Innovation" set underneath. Three sketch-level directions (original geometry — final art is a later drawing sprint). React to the ideas, not the exact curves.

A — Sideways B: two birds aloft

INNOVATION

The B lies on its side: its two bowls point up and become the classic two-humped gliding-bird silhouette, twice. Wittiest; closest to Aaron's stated idea. Risk: rotated-B legibility.

B — Bird-bowl B

INNOVATION

An upright, fully legible B whose two bowls are each a bird in profile — back/wing on the outer curve, a small beak notch toward the spine. Most conservative; birds discovered on second look.

C — Chevron-heritage 2B

INNOVATION

The B's bowls are the two chevron-birds from the current logo, vertex balls kept. Strongest continuity with the heritage mark; most complex to draw well.

Note: the v1.0 "two rising swallow-birds" mark from the first draft of this board was invented without knowledge of the real chevron logo — early exploration, superseded (kept in git history only).

The colour decision — logo blue #0066CC vs site teal #14b8a6

The real logo is blue; the live site, OG card, and v2 tokens are teal. Measured: teal on the blue = 2.99:1 (they cannot sit on each other); the blue on the site navy = 3.50:1 (UI-minimum only, fails as text). The v1.0 "keep teal + amber" call was made without seeing the logo — this three-way choice supersedes it and gates everything else.

Option 1 — Site aligns to logo blue

Two Birds Innovation
Blue becomes the brand primary
Book a call
  • Honours the mark exactly as drawn; one-hue brand
  • White on blue = 5.57:1 (AA) — logo accessible as-is
  • But: closest to default corporate/AI blue — Aaron's stated fear
  • Blue on the dark site navy = 3.50:1 — needs a light redesign or Sky Blue #4D94E0 (6.16:1) for UI

Option 2 — Coexistence (blue logo, teal site)

Two Birds Innovation
Blue tile mark · teal interface chrome
Book a call
  • Zero migration; both survive
  • But: adjacent cool hues at 2.99:1 against each other — side by side they read as an accident, not a system
  • Amber warmth accent also fails on the blue (2.99:1)
  • Weakest option long-term

Option 3 — Evolve the logo tile to navy

Two Birds Innovation
Same geometry + starfield, navy tile, teal accent ball optional
Book a call
  • Keeps the chevron geometry exactly; only the tile colour moves
  • One navy-and-teal universe: logo, site, OG, v2 tokens all cohere
  • Exits default-blue territory entirely — answers the stated fear
  • But: it modifies Aaron's real logo — genuinely his taste call, hence a lean (~55–60%), not a verdict
